## Introducing toust.so

toust.so is an open source saas platform that lets you add fake toast notifications to your website and manage them. It is built with Next.js, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, Shadcn, Auth.js, Vercel, and Prisma.

### Features

- Add fake toast notifications to your website.
- Manage & customize your toasts.
- Get a one liner code to add toust into your website in less than one minute.
- Share your toasts with others.
